# HR Policy Document — Company Handbook 2024

## Leave Policy
- Annual Leave: 18 paid leave days per year
- Casual Leave: 6 casual leave days per year  
- Sick Leave: 12 sick leave days per year
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks paid maternity leave
- Paternity Leave: 5 days paid paternity leave
- Leave encashment available for unused annual leave at year end

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week. Remote work requires manager approval. All remote employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST.

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Flexible start time allowed between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM with prior approval.

## Attendance Policy
- Employees must maintain 90% attendance per quarter
- Late arrivals beyond 15 minutes will be marked as half-day absence
- Three consecutive absences without notice triggers disciplinary review

## Salary & Payroll
- Salaries are disbursed on the last working day of each month
- Annual increments are processed in April each year
- Performance bonuses paid in Q4 based on KPI achievement

## Recruitment Policy
- All open positions must be posted internally for 7 days before external recruitment
- Referral bonus: ₹25,000 for successful permanent hire referrals
- Background verification mandatory for all new hires

## Code of Conduct
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or workplace violence
- Confidentiality of company information is mandatory
- Conflict of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately
- Social media policy: employees must not share confidential company data

## Performance Review
- Annual performance reviews conducted in March
- Mid-year check-ins conducted in September
- Performance Improvement Plans (PIP) issued for below-average performers
- Rating scale: Outstanding / Exceeds Expectations / Meets Expectations / Below Expectations

## Training & Development
- Each employee is entitled to ₹15,000 annual training budget
- Mandatory compliance training every 6 months
- Leadership development program available for senior staff
