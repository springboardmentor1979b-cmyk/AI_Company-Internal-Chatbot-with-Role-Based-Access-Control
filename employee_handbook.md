# Employee Handbook — General Policies 2024

## Welcome
Welcome to the company. This handbook outlines the general policies, benefits, and expectations for all employees regardless of department or seniority level.

## Office Locations
- Headquarters: Chennai, Tamil Nadu
- Branch offices: Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad
- All offices open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM

## Employee Benefits
- Health insurance: ₹5,00,000 family floater (employee + spouse + 2 children)
- Accidental insurance: ₹10,00,000 per employee
- Term life insurance: 3x annual salary
- Meal allowance: ₹3,500 per month
- Transport allowance: ₹2,000 per month
- Internet allowance (WFH): ₹1,000 per month

## General Leave Policy
- Total leave days per year: 36 (Annual + Casual + Sick combined)
- Public holidays: 12 days per year as per state government calendar
- Leave applications must be submitted 3 days in advance except emergencies
- Leave without pay (LWP) available after exhausting all leave types

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week as per company policy. Full remote work available only for certain approved roles with VP approval.

## Facilities
- Cafeteria available at all locations with subsidised meals
- Gymnasium available at HQ (6:00 AM to 9:00 PM)
- Free parking for employees at all locations
- Library and reading room at HQ

## IT & Equipment Policy
- Laptops provided to all employees (returned on resignation)
- Mobile phone allowance: ₹800/month for roles requiring field work
- Personal use of company equipment must be limited and professional
- VPN required for all remote access to company systems

## Grievance Policy
- All grievances must be reported to HR within 10 working days
- Anonymous complaint portal available on intranet
- Investigation completed within 30 working days
- Zero retaliation policy for good-faith grievance reporters

## Exit Policy
- Notice period: 30 days (junior), 60 days (senior/management)
- Full and final settlement within 45 days of last working day
- Experience letter issued within 7 days of exit
- NDAs remain in effect for 2 years post-employment

## Company Values
- Integrity: We do what we say
- Innovation: We challenge the status quo
- Inclusion: Every voice matters
- Impact: Results over activity
